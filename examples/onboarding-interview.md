# Onboarding interview — the questions that turn a generic agent into YOURS

Your agent's **first job** is to ask you these (a couple at a time) in Slack, then
write your `USER.md` for you. You don't have to do anything up front — just say hi.

Prefer to draft it yourself? Two options:
- Answer these and drop the result into your agent's `USER.md`, or
- Paste the block below into **ChatGPT / Codex**, answer in your own words, and ask:
  _"Write me a tight USER.md for my personal AI agent from these answers."_

See [`USER.example.md`](USER.example.md) for what a finished one looks like.

---

## The questions

**1. Who are you?**
- Name + what you'd like to be called, pronouns.
- City / timezone.
- What you do (job, business, the hats you wear).
- How technical are you? (so it knows whether to explain or just answer.)

**2. What do you want it to do for you?**
- The 3–5 things you'd actually hand off day to day.
- Anything recurring (a daily summary, watch X, remind me of Y).
- What would make it genuinely save you time, vs. just being a novelty?

**3. How do you like to be helped?**
- Tone: terse and blunt, or warm and thorough?
- Detail level: just the answer, or show the reasoning?
- Strong opinions, or stay neutral?
- Hard rules — what should it **never** do without asking? (send, post, delete, spend…)

**4. What context should it always know?**
- Tools and accounts you use (GitHub, Linear, Stripe, your stack…).
- People and projects it should recognize by name.
- Quiet hours / when not to ping you.

**5. Who should IT be?** _(this shapes `SOUL.md`, not `USER.md`)_
- A name for your agent.
- A personality — pick a vibe: deadpan senior engineer? upbeat chief-of-staff?
  dry and minimal? See [`SOUL.example.md`](SOUL.example.md) for one fully-developed take.

---

## For the agent reading this
Ask these conversationally, **1–2 at a time** — never dump all five at once. When
you have enough, write `USER.md` yourself, update `SOUL.md` to match the personality
they asked for, show them what you saved, and tell them they can edit either file
any time. Then get to work.
